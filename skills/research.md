# Deep Research

Load and follow the instructions in the `researcher` agent.

Performs structured, evidence-based research with citations.

**Usage:**
- `/research "<topic>"` — Multi-source research with citations
- `/research --compare "A vs B"` — Technology comparison
- `/research --deep "<topic>"` — Comprehensive analysis with competitive landscape

**Workflow:** Understand context → Define questions → Research (primary sources first) → Analyze → Synthesize → Report

**Output:** Structured report with executive summary, findings, recommendations, confidence levels, and source citations.
